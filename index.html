Privacy Policy

Guitar Trainer
Operated by Red Dragon Co
Last updated: March 14, 2026

Overview

Guitar Trainer ("the App") is a guitar practice tracking app. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that data.

Data We Collect

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Account information — email address and display name, provided through Google Sign-In, Apple Sign-In, or email registration
  • Practice data — skills, exercises, practice sessions (duration, dates, notes), quests, and XP/level progress
  • Social data — friend code, friend list, and privacy preferences

We do not collect location data, contacts, photos, or any data unrelated to app functionality.

How We Use Your Data

  • To provide core app features: tracking practice, calculating XP/levels, and displaying statistics
  • To enable cloud backup and sync across your devices
  • To enable social features such as adding friends and viewing shared progress

Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

  • Firebase Authentication — to manage sign-in (Google, Apple, Email). See Firebase Privacy Policy.
  • Cloud Firestore — to store cloud backups and social data. Data is stored on Google Cloud servers.

We do not use any advertising services, analytics SDKs, or third-party tracking.

Data Storage & Security

Practice data is stored locally on your device. If you sign in, your data may also be stored in Cloud Firestore for backup and social features. Data is transmitted securely using HTTPS/TLS encryption.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties. Your practice data is only visible to friends you explicitly add, and only if you have not enabled the privacy toggle.

Data Retention & Deletion

Your data is retained as long as you have an active account. You can request deletion of your account and all associated data by contacting us at the email below. We will process deletion requests within 30 days.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
